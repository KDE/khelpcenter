There are 4 ways to lock the screen:

  1. Click on the lockButton in the panel.
  2. Click on a empty space on your root window and press the right mouse button. A popup-menu will appear. Now click on 'Lock screen'.
  3. Just do it right now.
  4. Experts: Enter the command klock in a shell.You can get a prompt pressing Alt-F2
